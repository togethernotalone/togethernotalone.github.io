What is Together Not Alone?

Together Not Alone is an organization that focuses on the different issues and stigma pertaining to mental health. We are also a community that is dedicated to sharing people's stories about their experiences and journeys with mental health issues and illnesses.

What do we do?

Through our blogs and social media, we share experiences surrounding mental health issues and journeys. We also have discussion groups on topics surrounding mental health issues and illnesses. We provide information on resources available for those seeking help.

Why should you be involved?

We are a community with a common goal of bringing more awareness to mental health and the issues and illnesses associated with it. Being a part of Together Not Alone is not only a great way to become a voice for those battling mental health issues, but also a platform for leadership by participation in our discussion groups.
