> What is Together Not Alone?

A group of motivated, unstoppable girls who believe it's time for mental health to change.

> What is Together Not Alone?

This mental health organization was started by a group of determined Douglass women. Together Not Alone has actively supported and engaged the New Brunswick community, and went above and beyond the Rutgers University campus.
We are a community that is dedicated to altering the perception of mental health in the eyes of college students, minorities and local residents.

> How do we ignite the change?

Through our blogs and social media, we share real, impactful journeys of individuals about their struggles with mental health.

> Why should you be involved?

We also have discussion groups on topics surrounding mental health, such as depression and anxiety. We provide accurate, helpful resources to those seeking help.
