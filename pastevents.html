NAMI + TNA

On May 18, our co-directors (Monika and Akhila), along with our Event Coordinator (Sam) went to attend the NAMI Open House.

NAMI shares our vision of a future where mental illness is openly treated and accepted, rather than denied and brushed away.

If you are interested in NAMI and their vision, check out their website.

Rutgers Day 2019

On April 27 (Rutgers Day), the Together Not Alone team decided to increase our outreach by having a table for the public. We provided goodies for the little kids, along with positive quotes.

Through this experience, our organization had the chance to reach out to a huge, diverse population. As our nonprofit grows, we hope to attend more community-based events such as Rutgers Day!
